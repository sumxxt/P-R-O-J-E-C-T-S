Title : Lead Conversion Rate Analysis

Platform : Python-Pandas,numpy, ML,
Visualization- Seaborn, Matplotlib

Duration : 1 Month

Description : Lead Conversion Rate Analysis is to measure how effectively a company converts its qualified leads into actual
customers. The Lead Conversion Rate Analysis is to develop an analytical solution that helps businesses understand
and improve their lead conversion rate. The solution should help identify the key factors that influence lead
conversion, understand the relationship between these factors, and provide actionable insights to improve the lead
conversion rate. The goal of this case study was to build a model and help the company find hot leads based on
various attributes given in the dataset.

Dataset : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/amritachatterjee09/lead-scoring-dataset
